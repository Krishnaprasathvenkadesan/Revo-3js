About Us

About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, started in 1997, is an autonomous institution linked to Anna University. With 26 years of academic success, it's ranked the 3rd most preferred private engineering college in Tamil Nadu. The college offers 19 UG and 11 PG courses, plus programs in management, architecture, and nursing, producing skilled professionals every year.

About ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department started in 1997. It offers courses for UG, PG, and Ph.D. students. From the beginning, the department has focused on research, helping students become skilled researchers. Each year, over 95% of eligible students get placed in top companies. The department has been producing skilled engineers who can compete globally since it began.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the events?

Registration can be done through our online portal. Click on the 'Login' button at the top of the page, create an account, and follow the registration process for your desired events.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! You can participate in multiple events as long as they don't have timing conflicts. Check the event schedule carefully before registering for multiple events.

What should I bring on the event day?

Please bring your college ID, registration confirmation, and any specific materials required for your events. For technical events, laptops may be required. Event-specific requirements will be sent via email.

Is accommodation provided?

Yes, accommodation can be arranged upon request. Please mention your accommodation requirements during registration. Additional charges may apply.

Are there any team events?

Yes, several events allow team participation. Team size requirements vary by event. Check individual event details for specific team size limits and requirements.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, all participants will receive digital participation certificates. Winners will receive additional merit certificates and prizes.
